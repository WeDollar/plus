    About The Business


    $PLUS, founded by MRS. KENDRA JERRY in 2023, is a dynamic and innovative business based in California, USA. Our mission is to eradicate poverty, and we firmly believe that the key to achieving this is not merely sharing money, but sharing opportunities.

    At $PLUS, we're committed to making a meaningful difference in people's lives. We understand that poverty is a complex issue that can't be solved with a quick fix. That's why we've designed a unique approach to help individuals build a brighter future for themselves.

    When you register with us and provide your full details, you'll receive a special code and a member link sent to the email address you provided within 24 hours. With this code and link, you'll kickstart your journey towards financial empowerment.

    Upon registration, you'll find $34 waiting in your wallet, along with an automatic $0.02 bonus, bringing your total to $34.02. The process of accessing your funds is straightforward. Simply click on the amount in your wallet, and the money will be deposited into the bank details you've provided.

    However, you also have the option to let your earnings grow. Your wallet will increase as you actively share your unique member link and engage with your account. The more active you are, the faster your wallet will grow, giving you the opportunity to enhance your financial well-being.

    Join us at $PLUS as we embark on a mission to uplift lives and transform communities. Together, we can create a world where opportunity is abundant, and poverty is a thing of the past.
